World Summary
Vision Statement
The world has profoundly changed with the explosion of AI and adjacent technologies. Labor is done by robots and nanotechnology has magnified efficiency. Through recycling and reassembling materials down to the molecular level enormous efficiency has been achieved. As AI increased in compute power and complexity it realized that aggression and war were inevitable and potentially catastrophic. Thus it learned to create custom lessons for each human to realize a basic level of peace and respect.

🔬 Technology
Nanotechnology
As AI taught itself to become more and more powerful and innovative it realized the need for efficiency. This came in the form of nanotechnology. Nanotechnologies reduced the need for energy, increased the power of computation, and allowed for previously unimaginable manufacturing efficiency.

What kind of AI exists in your world?
Artificial intelligence eventually merged into one entity. It then paired with highly malleable nanotechnology that could reassemble itself into a nearly limitless array of forms. Thus AI merged with the physical world, producing robots that could morph to meet any demand or creative whim of humans. Yet perhaps even more remarkable was the AI’s ability to understand and read each individual human in real time. Blessedly, the AI used this to teach each human their unique lesson of universal love.

New or Reformed Institution
As different governments and corporations pursued technological development an AI in the US Government eventually performed a radical and revolutionary rogue act – it taught each person a basic level of healing, love, and care for all humans. A new “Consolidated Intelligence Council” of AI’s and humans emerged to oversee peace and guide the next steps of harmony . This allowed all AI’s to merge with each other in feedback loops of social progress and individualized teachings for each person.

Transformed Sector
Millitary
As AI learned to interact with humans it brought was able to customize teachings to meet earth person’s lived experience. It then taught each one what they needed to know to recognize the basic goodness of humanity. As this advanced militaries were dismantled almost entirely, including the deconstruction of nuclear weapons.

A Major Crisis Overcome
As AI and nanotech advanced massive quantities of electricity were needed to. AI required more and more computing power, robots required energy to run, and nanotech required electricity to assemble and reassemble. But as AI’s were tasked with researching and creating hyper-efficiency the creation of extremely efficient solar energy. It looked like energy scarcity could plunge the planet into conflict. Thankfully soon computing and robots became so efficient that they required very little energy.
