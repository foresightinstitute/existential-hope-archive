🌍 World Summary
Vision Statement
The Indefinite Lifespan: An AI/biotechnology interface that reverses aging and prolongs life indefinitely for each person on earth.

The Climate Stabilisation Biosphere: A geoengineered mastery of the natural elements that optimises biodiversity, nutritional crop yields, and negates any adverse effects of climate change/unpredictability while optimising conditions for an ever-expanding population.

🔬 Technology
The adoption of AI in biotechnological sciences
The number one problem of life being death, life will co-exist harmoniously in a non-violent, open society, where each individual has equal opportunity and ample time to create art, invent inventions, philosophise, participate in politics, build community, and enhance familiar and friendship bonds.

What kind of AI exists in your world?
AI is used to anticipate causes of death and prevent them through biotechnology and the creation of a stable biosphere mitigating any effects of climate disaster. AI is used to replace menial human tasks and is governed directly by a democratic process engaging each member on earth. The AI thus operates such as to creates equal opportunity for each person to freely benefit from the opportunities the AI apparatus creates- individually, in families, in communities, economically and politically.

New or Reformed Institution
The Indefinite Lifespan will be impossible without an association of geopolitical harmony and a healthy biosphere: A United Nations institution to realise world biosphere geoengineering goals as well as the fair implementation of the AI-accelerated biotechnology necessary to induce the indefinite lifespan. Non-violent cooperation between nations as well as between factions within nations will be necessary and emphasised in educational programs aiming to teach conflict resolution, reconciliation.

Transformed Sector
Healthcare
Healthcare has shifted primarily from treating deadly illnesses to preventing them with AI through “The Indefinite Lifespan” initiative. Healthcare in 2035 concerns itself now with amplifying each person’s psychological wellbeing and happiness, as well as on encouraging new ways for individuals to find meaningful activities in life and to prevent suicide, as individuals may still be suffering from psychological distress and coming to terms with AI’s powers of sustaining relative immortality.

A Major Crisis Overcome
Before 2035, this world faced massive job layoffs without a plan in place to make life economically viable for billions of workers worldwide who fell prey to divisive political tactics by an established ruling class indifferent to the plight of the lower and working classes. This had to be overcome by a democratisation of AI and cooperation at the level of global governance for individuals from all parts to buy into the goals of The Indefinite Lifespan and Stabilised Biosphere.
