🌍 World Summary
Vision Statement
In 2035, every person is welcomed into life with joy, care, and purpose, people are reconnected — to their bodies, to each other, and to the Earth. It’s a world where nobody feels like they were “not asked to be here.”

🔬 Technology
Bio-Responsive AI Interfaces
This technology enables people to communicate with the living world — from animals to forests — using AI that interprets bio-signals into emotional and sensory language. By translating the rhythms of nature, this tool helps individuals form lifelong bonds with other species

What kind of AI exists in your world?
AI is a relational tool, not a replacement. It’s used to bridge gaps—between people, between species, between inner experience and outer systems. Built by bio-ethicists, artists, and local communities, this AI helps us hear what was once inaudible: the voice of the river, the rhythm of the soil, the needs of our own bodies. It’s open-source, transparent, and evolves through collective wisdom.

New or Reformed Institution
The Welcome Circle
A global-local network that ensures every birth is recognized and celebrated by society. For each child, a guardianship ritual pairs them with a living being — a tree, a coral reef, a migrating bird — which they grow alongside. The Welcome Circle supports them in this journey, offering emotional mentorship, ecological education, and a sense of rootedness in both human and non-human kinship.

Transformed Sector
Health
Children and adults use wearable biomorphic tech that helps them understand, honor, and unlock their physical potential, not for performance, but for joy and connection. The Center for Embodied Wisdom  are local sanctuaries where people come to explore embodiment through food, touch, movement, rest, and shared presence — all with the gentle support of AI-guided tools. Each center also trains Somatic Stewards who help communities integrate this tech with cultural traditions and personal dignity.

A Major Crisis Overcome
The Crisis of Disconnection
For too long, people felt unwanted, unsupported, and severed from nature. Mental illness surged, families collapsed under pressure, and young people lost purpose. The turning point came when communities reimagined what it means to raise a human — not as property or burden, but as a shared celebration. This shift, powered by a new kind of AI and new social rituals, has reconnected us to life — and to each other.
